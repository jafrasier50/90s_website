My Focus?

I know that when I mentioned that I was "all over the place" in
terms of what I wanted wanted to do with my life. If I'm being
honest, I still don't know if this is it, but I'm commited to seeing
it through. It really helps that I like this. Maybe this is what I'm
cut out for.

So, what IS my focus?

My focus is to leave an impression. Not the just any, but the biggest
I'm capable of. Whether it was serving that last table at 9:58pm on a
Friday night, Waking up before the crack of down to open the store for
breakfast, or having a sleepless night catching up and going through
at Headquarters, I want to leave an impression that someone will say
something about.

Part of that focus is building relations with all those around me.
I believe it is relations that take someones oppertunities to the
next level.

"It's not only about what you know. It's who you know."
